How to Play

Player 1 (Green)

Move: W, A, S, D

Shoot: Space

Player 2 (Blue)

Move: Arrow Keys

Shoot: Enter

On mobile, use the on-screen controls to move and shoot.

Tap the "Show Mobile Controls" button to display touch controls.

Collect beads to grow longer. Shoot to knock beads off your opponent.

The life counter shows how many segments each caterpillar has. When a player reaches zero lives, they lose!

Use the speed slider to adjust how fast the caterpillars move.

Warning: When a player is down to their last segment, it will pulse red!
